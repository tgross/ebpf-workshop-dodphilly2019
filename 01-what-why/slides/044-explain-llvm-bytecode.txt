* ~LLVM~: A compiler toolchain. Used to compile C, C++, Objective C,
  Julia, Swift, and Rust, among many others. Used to compile BPF
  programs into BPF bytecode.

* ~BPF_PROG_LOAD~: A syscall used to load a BPF program into the BPF
  VM in the kernel.

* ~BPF_MAP_LOOKUP_ELEM~: One of many syscalls used to get information
  out of the BPF VM and into userland.
