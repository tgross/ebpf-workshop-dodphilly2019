% BPF


* ~The BSD Packet Filter: A New Architecture for User-level Packet~
  ~Capture~ (Steve McCanne and Van Jacobson 1992)

* Virtual Machine to work efficiently with register based CPUs

* Packet filtering without copying data

* Ex: ~tcpdump~, ~wireshark~

* Today we call this classic BPF or ~cBPF~
